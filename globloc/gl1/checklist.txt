GL1 experimenter checklist


PREPARE THE LABS
- front door to lab is open
- computer is on and signed in
- light iin cubicle is on the brightest setting
- computer screen is at the lowest position and at lowest position on the stand, and lined up at 12 inch tape mark on desk
- chair is in a natural place so they can enter their demographics
- keyboard mask is nearby (labelled labA and labB)
- candy, sanitizer, tissues on desk

PREPARE GLOBLOC1
- set directory to ~/specl-exp/data2/globloc/gl1
- run: python gl1.py
- check if the experiment is ' gl1' 

PARTICIPANT COMES IN
- check if they are who signed up
- irb consent
- let participant sign in and give demographics
- move chair to critical position 
- give instructions to not move the chair around and keep keyboard on lap
- place keyboard mask
- Explain experiment.  
  a. big letter made of little letters
  b. for a group of trials, a block, we ask you to identify the big letter
  c. for another group, the small letter
  d. always use the two keys provided
  e. try to be accurate, if you make a mistake you get a 1 second time out
  f. 6 blocks total, feel free to rest between blocks as long as you wish
  g.  takes about 20 minutes, tops.
  h.  tell them there will be a note on the screen when they are done
  h.  thank them again for their efforts
- stay for a few practice trials, then shut the door


AFTER

- lead them out to the main room, ask them to wait
- Press 9 to end
- note any comments or concerns in final dialog screen
- close the experiment screen
- debrief participant with information about experiment
a. ask how they found the task, which was the hardest letter global local combo
b. our goal was does everyone do the same thing.
c. smaller letter is harder than the global for some, is it harder for all.
d. are there people who find it harder to exclude global or local info?
